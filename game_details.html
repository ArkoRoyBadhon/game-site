Description:

The original, remastered! One of our first games as a studio, and still a top performer, we decided to give ‘Book of Souls’ a big update with wonderful new graphics, much-improved symbols, cleaner UI and an awesome soundscape. Book Of SoulsTM Remastered is an action-packed, 5×3 videoslot that follows the famous archaeologist-turned-treasure hunter, Lara Jones, on her a quest to use the mystical Book Of Souls to unlock the fabled riches hidden inside a giant Mayan tomb, deep in the jungle. This is a high volatility game with exciting features such as Snake Spins, which drop random wilds onto the reels, and a special expanding symbol during the free spin mode.
